Our Story

TechBhel started as a crazy idea during a late-night coding session in 2023. Four developers, fueled by nothing but instant noodles and caffeine, realized that programmers needed better food options that understood their lifestyle.

"What if we could serve street food that's as well-structured as our code?" asked our founder, Dev Sharma, while debugging his 100th line of JavaScript. And thus, TechBhel was born - a place where every recipe is version-controlled, every dish is unit-tested for taste, and every customer leaves with zero hunger bugs!
